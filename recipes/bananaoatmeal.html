Banana Oatmeal

This warm hearty banana oatmeal is perfect for a winter morning.

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup old fashioned oats
  • 1/3 cup milk of your choice
  • 2/3 or 1/2 cup water depending on your preference of the consistency
  • 1/2 banana very thinly sliced
  • Pinch of kosher salt
  • A handful of any toppings you like, optional

Steps:

  1. Combine oats, water, and milk in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Slice a banana into VERY thin pieces, leaving just a few bigger chunks.
  3. Once it begins to steam or bubble, stir vigorously at times to “whip” the banana into the oats.
  4. The oats will take about 5 minutes on medium heat from start to finish to fully absorb the liquid.
  5. Remove from hear when they reach your desired consistency.
