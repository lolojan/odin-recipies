Tomato Pasta

Tomato Pasta is a classic Italian dish. It is healthy, delicious and quick to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 200g of spaghettini pasta
  • A pint of cherry tomatoes, sliced
  • A handful of basil leaves
  • 6 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 4 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste, salt to season pasta water
  • ⅓ cup of reserved hot pasta water or water

Steps:

  1. Prepare the ingredients as listed above.
  2. Boil the pasta according to package instructions in a pot of salted water.
  3. Reserve ⅓ cup of hot pasta water for sauce.
  4. Meanwhile, in a large pan set on low heat, add your olive oil. Add in garlic and slow fry until fragrant.
  5. Once fragrant, add in the tomatoes. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Fry until tomatoes are softened.
  6. Cover with a lid and let it cook for about 3-5 minutes.
  7. Remove the lid and add in reserved pasta water. Then throw in the cooked pasta.
  8. Top off with chopped basil leaves and combine well.
  9. Once combined, turn off your heat and plate your pasta.
